## 🚀 App Store Listing

### **App Name**

### **Short Description (Summary)**
The premium screen capture tool for pros. Instantly capture, stitch, and export full webpages to PDF or PNG. Smart handling for sticky headers and scrollable apps.

### **Long Description**

**Capture Your Digital Journey with Gnomad Capture.**

Stop struggling with fragmented screenshots. Gnomad Capture is the professional-grade browser extension designed for developers, designers, and power users who need pixel-perfect full-page captures.

Whether you're archiving a landing page, sharing a design mockup, or saving a receipt, Gnomad Capture handles the complexity so you don't have to.

**🌟 Key Features:**

*   **📸 Smart Full-Page Stitching**: Automatically scrolls and stitches long web pages into a single, high-resolution image.
*   **🧠 Intelligent Element Handling**: Detects and temporarily hides sticky headers, footers, and floating widgets so they don't clutter your screenshot.
*   **🛠️ Built-in Editor**: Crop, annotate, and redact sensitive information using the intuitive redaction tool before saving.
*   **Cc: Copy to Clipboard**: One-click copy to paste directly into Slack, email, or documents.
*   **📄 Professional PDF Export**: Save as a seamless single-page PDF or a perfectly paginated A4 document ready for printing.
*   **🔒 Privacy First**: All processing happens locally in your browser. No servers, no tracking, no data leaks.
*   **🎨 Premium Design**: A sleek, dark-mode interface that feels right at home in your modern workflow.

**Why Gnomad Capture?**

Unlike other tools that result in blurry, cut-off, or squashed images, Gnomad Capture uses advanced DOM detection to find the *true* scrollable area of modern web apps (like SPAs and dashboards). It ensures your captures look exactly like the real thing—every time.

**No Watermarks. No Account Required. Just Capture.**
